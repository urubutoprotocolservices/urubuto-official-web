About Us

Urubuto Protocol & Services is a professional event support company dedicated to helping individuals, organizations, and institutions create well-organized and memorable events.


We provide professional protocol services including guest reception, event coordination, seating arrangements, VIP assistance, and overall event support to ensure smooth and successful occasions.


Our services cover a wide range of events including weddings, marriage ceremonies, thanksgiving events, graduations, conferences, workshops, trainings, study tours, and tourism-related activities.


We operate across Kigali City and all districts of the Southern Province of Rwanda, bringing professional and reliable protocol services closer to our clients wherever their events take place.
